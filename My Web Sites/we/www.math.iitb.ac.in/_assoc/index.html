About the Society

History

The Mathematics Society of IIT Bhu was formed in 1978 as a student body organization aimed at bringing awareness, generate and promote interest, and facilitate communication among students of various age groups with an interest in Mathematics.

Target

Targeted at motivating and encouraging student participation, this Society aims to host activities at national level in the coming years. The quickly growing popularity of the Society is not only a success for the Society but for Mathematics itself.

Overview

The Society is responsible for the different mathematical activities that takes place round the year in the department. Society tries to foster the development of mathematics inquisitiveness among students of IIT and students around Varanasi through various events and workshops.

Our Work


Mathematics Olympiad

The most important and widespread activity of the Society is the Mathematics Olympiad, a competitive examination, conducted at the end of January or the start of February each year.

Student Lectures

The Society also organizes,a Popular Lecture Series by eminent Mathematicians. This allows a direct link between students and Mathematicians doing work at the very cutting edge. Recent developments and applications in technology never fail to interest a plethora of students. This highlights the importance of these lecture series.

About IIT Bhu


The Indian Institute of Technology Bhu (abbreviated IITB or IIT Bhu) is a public engineering institution located in Varanasi, India. In global rankings, IIT Bhu has emerged as India’s top university.
